Privacy Policy for Vuddy

Effective date: February 14, 2026

Vuddy is a student assistant app. If you choose to connect Google Calendar, Vuddy requests read-only access to your calendar events to show your upcoming schedule inside the app.

What We Collect

  • OAuth authorization data needed to access your Google Calendar.
  • Calendar event data you choose to import (title, time, location, notes).
  • Basic app usage data needed to run core features.

How We Use Data

  • To display your events inside Vuddy.
  • To support reminders, scheduling, and event recommendations.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data. Data is only shared with services required for app functionality, such as Google APIs that you authorize.

Data Retention

Imported calendar data may remain in app storage until deleted. You can revoke access at any time in your Google Account permissions.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data. No system is guaranteed to be perfectly secure.

Your Choices

  • You can choose not to connect Google Calendar.
  • You can revoke Google access at any time.
